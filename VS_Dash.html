Dash

Tier 1 • Common Spell

Dash, jump, or fly for a short distance. An invaluable spell for scouts and skirmishers.

Magically propel yourself 5+Tm through the air.

If you have a target of equal size Grabbed, you can dash with them. If you have Grabbed a larger target, you can knock them down. Resist : ♣♠Athletics/Endure vs Tier

Enhanced : Escape as a part of this spell. When escaping Grabs or Restraint, use your Spell Roll in place of ♣♠Athletics

Wind Dash Tier 1 • Air • Dash

[◇] A great gust of wind carries you 5+Tier meters. Gain Cover this round. Cover protects from Range/Burst attacks

Enhanced : Glide, only falling 3m at the end of your turn.

Rocket Jump Tier 1 • Ballistic • Dash

(◆) Create a small explosion, launching yourself 5+Tier meters in any direction. Anyone within 1m of your starting point is Knocked Down and takes 1d6/Tier Crush damage

Save : ♣♠Ath/End vs Tier (+Advantage if target is larger)

Force Dash Tier 1 • Channel • Dash

[◇] Empower your legs, sprinting or leaping 5+T meters. If you hit a target (or if you had already grabbed a target), you may immediately make a free Brawl Attack against them using your Spell Roll.

Enhanced : Gain +Advantage on Athletics rolls this round.

Brawl Attacks include Grab, Push, Knockdown, Pummel (unarmed attack), Interpose (prevent action)

Slipstream Tier 1 • Distortion • Dash

Create and slip through a portal, leading to a space you can see. The portal only lasts for this action, and others can't follow you through it. Can't be used to Escape Grab or Restraint.

[◆] Warp 6m away, then Move

[◇] Warp 3m away, then Move

[↻] Enhanced : Warp 3m away, evading an attack. Roll Spell Roll vs Attack Roll to successfully evade an attack, or use Spell Roll to resist a Burst Attack. You must be able to warp out of an attack's range to avoid it.

Catapult Tier 1 • Earth • Dash

(◆) Launch a target within 6m of yourself (Medium or smaller) up to 5+Tier meters off an earthen surface. Unwilling targets Save ♠♦Perception vs Tier

Enhanced: Launch 2 additional targets

Enhanced: Launch an object; roll ♦Aim vs ♠Per to hit a target for 2+Size(3) +Siege Crush damage. Target has Advantage if in cover.

Mirror Step Tier 1 • Illusion • Dash

[◇] An illusory copy of yourself sprints 5+Tier meters in any direction, triggering traps and Free Attacks

Enemies with Extrasense can Save ♠Perception vs Spell to recognize the illusion

Enhanced: The illusory copy can make a Melee Attack at the end of its run, dealing Tier(Skill) Pain damage (Spell Roll vs Defense)

Mirror Step can be used to escape Grabs (♦Perception vs Tier), but does not help with Restraint
